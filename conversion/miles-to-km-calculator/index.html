What This Calculator Does

This miles to kilometers calculator converts a distance in miles into kilometers using the standard conversion formula.

It is useful for travel planning, running and walking distances, road trips, and understanding metric distance measurements.

Miles to Kilometers Formula

To convert miles to kilometers, multiply the number of miles by 1.60934.

Formula: Kilometers = Miles × 1.60934

Example Calculation

If you enter 10 miles:

10 × 1.60934 = 16.0934 kilometers

So, 10 miles equals 16.09 kilometers.
