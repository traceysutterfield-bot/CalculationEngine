What This Calculator Does

This kilometers to miles calculator converts a distance in kilometers into miles using the standard conversion formula.

It is useful for travel planning, running and walking distances, road trips, and understanding imperial distance measurements.

Kilometers to Miles Formula

To convert kilometers to miles, multiply the number of kilometers by 0.621371.

Formula: Miles = Kilometers × 0.621371

Example Calculation

If you enter 10 kilometers:

10 × 0.621371 = 6.21371 miles

So, 10 kilometers equals 6.21 miles.
