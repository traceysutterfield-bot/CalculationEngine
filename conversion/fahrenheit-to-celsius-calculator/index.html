What This Calculator Does

This Fahrenheit to Celsius calculator converts a temperature in Fahrenheit into Celsius using the standard temperature conversion formula.

It is useful for weather conversions, cooking temperatures, science calculations, and understanding temperature measurements used in different countries.

Fahrenheit to Celsius Formula

To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature and then multiply by 5/9.

Formula: Celsius = (Fahrenheit − 32) × 5/9

Example Calculation

If you enter 77 degrees Fahrenheit:

(77 − 32) × 5/9 = 25°C

So, 77°F equals 25°C.
