What This Calculator Does

This Celsius to Fahrenheit calculator converts a temperature in Celsius into Fahrenheit using the standard temperature conversion formula.

It is useful for weather conversions, cooking temperatures, science calculations, and understanding temperature measurements used in different countries.

Celsius to Fahrenheit Formula

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5 and then add 32.

Formula: Fahrenheit = (Celsius × 9/5) + 32

Example Calculation

If you enter 25 degrees Celsius:

(25 × 9/5) + 32 = 77°F

So, 25°C equals 77°F.
