What This Calculator Does

This calculator finds the result of taking a percentage of a number (written as “X% of Y”). It’s commonly used for discounts, tips, tax, commissions, and everyday percentage calculations.

The formula is: (Percent ÷ 100) × Number. Example: 20% of 150 = (20 ÷ 100) × 150 = 30.
